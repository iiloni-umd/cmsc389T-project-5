About Me

Hello! Welcome to my digital portfolio. I am Itai Iloni, a Computer Science and Psychology double major at the University of Maryland. My primary academic interests include data science and analysis, programming languages, data structures, cognitive psychology, and industrial-organizational psychology. This combination of majors is not common, and may not seem entirely intuitive at a glance - but there is more than what meets the eye. In computer science, it takes more than technical skills to build a successful program or design - you need an understanding of how people interact with software, which is where Psychology can give a helping hand. On the other side of things, Psychology is a data-driven field that more often than not can make use of the powerful computation ability of modern data analysis to make effective decisions in the workplace or research lab.
